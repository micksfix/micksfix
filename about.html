The student of the swing

Somewhere along the way, playing wasn’t enough — I wanted to understand why the swing works. That led me deep into biomechanics, the kinematic sequence, and how the body generates and transfers energy without breaking down. I co-founded a golf venture built around a movement-based methodology, and later worked in swing-analysis technology, including markerless 3D motion capture. That blend of playing experience and movement science is the foundation of how I teach today.

The teacher

Today I teach in the Irvine area of Southern California, working with golfers of every age and level and continuing my path toward PGA of America Class A membership. I teach in both English and Japanese. My focus is simple: help each golfer build a swing that fits their body and holds up — not a copy of someone else’s.
