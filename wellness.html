What it is

PEMF stands for Pulsed Electromagnetic Field therapy. In plain terms, it uses gentle magnetic pulses to support your body at the cellular level — the same kind of natural fields your body already responds to, delivered in a focused, consistent way. No needles, no medication, no downtime. Most of the time you’re simply resting comfortably while the device does its work.
