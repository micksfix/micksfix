What is PEMF?

PEMF stands for Pulsed Electromagnetic Field therapy. In simple terms, it uses gentle, pulsed magnetic fields to stimulate activity at the cellular level — the same kind of fields the body is naturally exposed to, just delivered in a more targeted, consistent way.

There’s no needles, no medication, and no downtime. Most sessions involve simply lying near or on a PEMF device while it runs through a set program.

Why people use it

People come to PEMF for different reasons — supporting recovery after physical activity, managing everyday tension, or simply as part of a broader wellness routine. It’s not a cure for anything, and it’s not a replacement for medical care. It’s a tool that some people find genuinely helpful as part of how they take care of themselves.
